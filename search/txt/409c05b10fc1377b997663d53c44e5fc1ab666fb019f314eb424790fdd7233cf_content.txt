It has a climbing zone at the toe and ensures good edging performance. Its POMOCA Butylic compound rubber provides better grip on rock in both dry and wet conditions, while the aggressive tread lugs offer improved traction on mud and softer terrain. We make the precise-fitting upper from robust mesh and give it an EXA Shell technology injected 3D cage for enhanced torsional stability. It works together with our 3F system to wrap your foot for flexibility, fit and firm ankle and heel support. To cope with wet conditions, the Wildfire GTX has a GORE-TEX® Extended Comfort lining to provide waterproof and breathable protection. Climbing lacing further supports the wrapping fit, especially at the forefoot and allows you to fine-tune the toe for greater precision in technical terrain.
