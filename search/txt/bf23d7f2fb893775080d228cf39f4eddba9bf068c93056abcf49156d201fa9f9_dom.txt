<h1><span style="font-size: medium;"><strong>Postdoctoral Position in Chemistry</strong></span></h1>
</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 5px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px;">
<div style="font-family: customcf844c2f820444f1a3ffc; font-size: 15px;">
<div>
<div><span style="font-size: small;">Faculty/Services:&nbsp; <span lang="en-GB" xml:lang="en-GB" data-careersite-propertyid="dept">Faculty of Science </span></span></div>

</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px;">
<div style="font-family: customcf844c2f820444f1a3ffc; font-size: 15px;">
<div>
<div><span style="font-size: small;">Educational level:&nbsp; <span lang="en-GB" xml:lang="en-GB" data-careersite-propertyid="customfield1">PhD </span></span></div>

</div>

<div style="font-family: customcf844c2f820444f1a3ffc; font-size: 15px;">
<div>
<div><span style="font-size: small;">Function type:&nbsp; <span lang="en-GB" xml:lang="en-GB" data-careersite-propertyid="customfield2">Academic Staff </span></span></div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 5px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px;">
<div style="font-family: customcf844c2f820444f1a3ffc; font-size: 15px;">
<div>
<div><span style="font-size: small;">Closing date:&nbsp; <span lang="en-GB" xml:lang="en-GB" data-careersite-propertyid="customfield3">01.08.2024 </span></span></div>

</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 5px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px;">
<div style="font-family: customcf844c2f820444f1a3ffc; font-size: 15px;">
<div>
<div><span style="font-size: small;">Vacancy number:&nbsp; <span lang="en-GB" xml:lang="en-GB" data-careersite-propertyid="customfield4">13149 </span></span></div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 5px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px;">
<div style="font-family: customcf844c2f820444f1a3ffc; font-size: 15px;">
<div>
<div>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">The <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a href="https://hims.uva.nl/" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">Van 't Hoff Institute for Molecular Sciences</span></a></span> (HIMS) is one of eight institutes of the <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a href="https://www.uva.nl/en/about-the-uva/organisation/faculties/faculty-of-science/faculty-of-science.html" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">Faculty of Science</span></a></span> of the University of Amsterdam. (UvA). HIMS performs internationally recognized research in molecular sciences, both curiosity as well as application driven. This is partly done in cooperation with the chemical, energy, flavour &amp; food, medical and high-tech industries. Within the Synthesis and catalysis cluster, the Organic Chemistry Group (SOC) is looking for a postdoctoral researcher. The postdoctoral researcher will perform the research in the laboratory of the SOC group in collaboration with AstraZeneca (dr. Magnus Johansson).</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">What are you going to do</span></span></p>
<p><br><span style="font-size: small;"> <span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">The project "C-H Methylation for late-stage drug discovery" is a joint project of the University of Amsterdam (UvA) and AstraZeneca, funded by Chemistry NL. Symeres is also involved in this collaboration. Our goal is to develop general C-H methylation reactions that will allow the direct introduction of methyl groups into a number of lead compounds in order to significantly increase their biological potency.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">What do we require</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">A PhD degree in organic chemistry and catalysis;</span></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">You should be a creative thinker that likes challenging projects;</span></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">You have a background in organic synthesis and catalysis;</span></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">You should be team players as the projects will be carried out in the context of a collaborative program;</span></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">You should have excellent project- and time management skills;</span></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">You should have excellent communication skills; for presenting data in conferences and workshops and to potential partners;</span></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">fluency in English, both written and spoken.</span></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">You will be appointed at the University of Amsterdam (UvA).<br> &nbsp;</span></span><br><span style="font-size: small;"> <span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">Your experience and profile</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">A PhD degree in organic chemistry and catalysis with substantial experience in metal-catalyzed C-H functionalization processes is required;</span></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">The ability to collaborate and adapt in an international team is required and candidates must have good communication skills in English, both written and oral.</span></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">Due to limitations in recruitment budget, we will preferably consider candidates that are currently in Europe.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">The position is open until we find a proper canditate.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">Our offer</span></span></p>
<p><br><span style="font-size: small;"> <span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">A temporary contract for 38 hours per week, preferably starting as soon as possible, for the duration of 12 months. An extension may be possible based on available funding combined with adequate performance.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">The gross monthly salary, based on 38 hours per week and dependent on relevant experience, ranges between € 3,226 - € 5,090 (scale 10). This does not include 8% holiday allowance and 8,3% year-end allowance. The UFO profile Researcher 4 is applicable. A favourable tax agreement, the ‘30% ruling’, may apply to non-Dutch applicants.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">The <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a href="https://www.universiteitenvannederland.nl/en/collective-labour-agreement-of-dutch-universities" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">Collective Labour Agreement of Dutch Universities</span></a></span> is applicable.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">Besides the salary and a vibrant and challenging environment at Science Park we offer you multiple fringe benefits:<br> •&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;232 holiday hours per year (based on fulltime) and extra holidays between Christmas and 1 January;<br> •&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;multiple courses to follow from our Teaching and Learning Centre;<br> •&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;multiple courses on topics such as leadership for academic staff;<br> •&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;multiple courses on topics such as time management, handling stress and an online learning platform with 100+ different courses;<br> •&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;7 weeks birth leave (partner leave) with 100% salary;<br> •&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;partly paid parental leave;<br> •&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;the possibility to set up a workplace at home;<br> •&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;a pension at ABP for which UvA pays two third part of the contribution;<br> •&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;the possibility to follow courses to learn Dutch;<br> Are you curious to read more about our extensive package of secondary employment benefits, take a look <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a href="https://www.uva.nl/en/about-the-uva/working-at-the-uva/working-at-the-uva.html" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">here</span></a></span>.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">About us</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">The Van 't Hoff Institute for Molecular Sciences (HIMS) is one of eight institutes of the <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a href="https://www.uva.nl/en" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">University of Amsterdam</span></a></span> (UvA) Faculty of Science. HIMS performs internationally recognized chemistry and molecular research, curiosity driven as well as application driven. This is done in close cooperation with the chemical, flavor &amp; food, medical and high-tech industries. Research is organized into four themes: <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a href="https://hims.uva.nl/content/research-groups/analytical-chemistry/analytical-chemistry.html" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">Analytical Chemistry</span></a></span>, <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a href="https://hims.uva.nl/content/research-groups/computational-chemistry/computational-chemistry.html" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">Computational Chemistry</span></a></span>, <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a href="https://hims.uva.nl/research/research-themes/synthesis--catalysis.html" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">Synthesis &amp; Catalysis</span></a></span> and <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a href="https://hims.uva.nl/content/research-groups/molecular-photonics/molecular-photonics.html" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">Molecular Photonics</span></a></span>.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">The <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a href="https://www.fernandezibanezgroup.com/" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">Fernández-Ibánez Research Group</span></a></span>, which is part of <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a href="https://hims.uva.nl/content/research-groups/synthetic-organic-chemistry/synthetic-organic-chemistry.html" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">The Synthetic Organic Chemistry Group</span></a></span> aims at the development of efficient synthetic methodologies for the synthesis of added value chemicals and materials. In several research projects they cooperate with national and international chemical and pharmaceutical companies, sharing their expertise.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">The <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a href="https://www.uva.nl/en" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">University of Amsterdam</span></a></span> is the Netherlands' largest university, offering the widest range of academic programmes. At the UvA, 30,000 students, 6,000 staff members and 3,000 PhD candidates study and work in a diverse range of fields, connected by a culture of curiosity.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">The Faculty of Science has a student body of around 8,000, as well as 1,800 members of staff working in education, research or support services. Researchers and students at the Faculty of Science are fascinated by every aspect of how the world works, be it elementary particles, the birth of the universe or the functioning of the brain.&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">To work at the University of Amsterdam is to work in a discerning, independent, creative, innovative and international climate characterized by an open atmosphere and a genuine engagement with the city of Amsterdam and society. <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a href="https://www.uva.nl/en/about-the-uva/working-at-the-uva/working-at-the-uva.html" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">Here</span></a></span>&nbsp;you can read more about working at the University of Amsterdam.</span></span></p>
